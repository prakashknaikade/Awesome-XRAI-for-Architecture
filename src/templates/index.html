How to Use Filters & Features

Search

Type keywords, titles, or author names in the search box. Click to clear.

Category & Year

Use the dropdowns to quickly narrow papers down by primary category (publication theme) or year.

Interactive Tags

Click tags once to Include (blue), twice to Exclude (red), and a third time to clear the filter.

Paper Cards

Each paper displays its primary category (green) on the top right, followed by sub-categories (amber) and additional theme tags.

Selection Mode

Toggle the list icon in the floating menu, check paper rows to add to your list, and click Share to generate custom links.

Analytics Dashboard

Click the analytics link in the floating menu to view statistics, trends, and charts for the paper library.

Tag Hierarchy & Filtering

Tags are grouped under primary categories (green) and sub-categories (amber). Selecting a category dropdown filters the available tags and years automatically to show only associated items.
